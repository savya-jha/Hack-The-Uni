About Hack The Uni

Hack The Uni is an aggregator of life hacks for university students. A life hack is any technique or way of doing things that allows its user to simplify their actions and achieve their goals more easily. Here, students can find life hacks related to studying and test preparation, work and money, exercising as well as routine chores like cleaning or cooking.

Hack The Uni is committed to bringing high quality content that makes the lives of its users simpler. Should you wish to write for us or have your blog post featured here, get in touch with us here about contributing to our mission.
